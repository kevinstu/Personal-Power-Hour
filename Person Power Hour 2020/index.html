How to Play

1.

Select six musical categories from the Category List below

2.

Hit the "Let's Party" button to lock in categories and start the timer

3.

At the 60 minute mark, one person plays a song that fits the category

(You will need a device with Spotify, Apple Music, Youtube, etc.)

4.

At the 59 minute mark exactly, the next person plays their song

5.

Repeat until the hour is complete

6.

Switch categories every 10 Minutes

Drinking Rules

7.

Take a sip of your drink every single minute

8.

If you don't play your song at the minute mark, finish your drink.

9.

One person in the group must know the song. If not, finish your drink.

10.

Cheers out loud to every artist you pick. If you forget, finish your drink.

(For example, "Cheers to Lil Jon and the East Side Boys!")

11.

If you repeat a musician that someone has already played, finish your drink

12.

You can challenge anyone's song pick, if you dont think it fits the category.

Majority vote wins. Loser of the challenge has to finish their drink
